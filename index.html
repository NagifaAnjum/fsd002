Smart Agriculture System

What is Smart Agriculture System?

Introduction:

Agriculture and human existence are inextricably linked to each other. From the beginning of the social system, a country's economy is explicitly or implicitly engaged in agriculture. One of the foremost elements of agriculture is soil. The constituents of soil determine the fertility of any land. The soil of assorted vicinity diverges depending on its topography. Equate harvest can not be gained from all types of soil. Depending on soil moisture, PH scale, and temperature, different types of soil are suitable for different crops and cereals.
